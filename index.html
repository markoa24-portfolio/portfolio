01 — About

Aerospace Engineer|

I’m Mark Agban (B.S. Aerospace Engineering, UCLA). I work end-to-end on mechanical systems with a bias toward manufacturability, robustness, and test. Recent work includes a from-scratch live-axle go-kart — square-tube weldment chassis, 212 cc electric-start powerplant, 30-series torque converter, and a custom push-to-start — plus contributions to a high-power rocket and a porous-airfoil study at USC.

Core: SolidWorks · MATLAB · ANSYS · GD&T · DFM & manufacturing planning · mechanical/electrical systems integration · test planning & data analysis
