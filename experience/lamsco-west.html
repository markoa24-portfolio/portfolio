Lamsco West — Manufacturing Engineering Intern

July 2025 – September 2025

Overview

In Summer 2025, I worked as a Manufacturing Engineering Intern at Lamsco West, a leading supplier of laminated and bonded shims for aerospace. My role focused on interpreting prime specifications, resolving manufacturing issues, and coordinating across teams to ensure compliance with the requirements of top aerospace companies.

Key Contributions

Specification Reviews

  • Applied and interpreted specifications and drawings from Boeing, Lockheed Martin, Raytheon, and Northrop Grumman.
  • Ensured compliance across multiple processes — machining, laminating, plating, and inspection — by validating requirements before production and during in-process checks.

Issue Resolution

  • Supported resolution of manufacturing challenges including waviness, delamination, and tool wear.
  • Developed solutions that balanced shop-floor constraints with the strict standards of aerospace quality.
  • Worked with engineers and quality to document corrective actions through root-cause analyses and compliance reports.

Cross-Functional Coordination

  • Engaged with teams at all levels of the company — from production operators to management and executives — to translate complex aerospace prime specifications into clear, executable processes.
  • Collaborated with buyers, suppliers, and vendors when external coordination was needed to meet spec or delivery requirements.
