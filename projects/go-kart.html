Custom Go-Kart (live axle)

Square-tube weldment chassis, 212 cc ES engine, TAV-30, in-house fabrication, custom push-to-start.

Overview

Single-seat, live-axle kart designed for reliability and serviceability. The chassis is a 1×1×.120″ A513 square-tube weldment; power is a 212 cc four-stroke with electric-start; the drivetrain uses a 30-series torque converter to #420 chain on a 1″ live axle. CAD validated ergonomics, chainline, brake & steering clearances, and cable/harness routing. A custom push-to-start was integrated to the ES circuit and a coil kill-switch added for safety. Fabrication done in-house (chop saw + angle grinder) with kerf-aware cut lists, tack sequence planning, and simple fixturing.

Highlights

  • CAD → cuts: SolidWorks weldments; drawings & cut list (kerf/tolerance accounted).
  • Weldment strategy: balanced tack sequence and checkpoints for squareness.
  • Integration: chainline, steering geometry, brake mount & alignment, ES push-to-start wiring.
  • Serviceability: access for chain service, brake bleeding/pads, and engine removal.
